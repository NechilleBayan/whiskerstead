# Assets — Props & Items

## Specs
- Canvas: **64×64 px** authoring (displays ~24–32 px). Export 2× and 1×. PNG RGBA, transparent.
- Must be identifiable at 24 px: bold silhouette, 1–2 colors, thick doodle outline.
- Every carriable item needs to sit naturally in the carry-walk paw slot (consistent anchor point, item angled ~15°).
