## Personality Archetypes (decision-weight flavor + conflict source)

| Archetype | Core trait | Source of conflict | Cult tendency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Planner | Wants everything organized | Gets annoyed by everyone | Joins when structure appears; may reform/take over |
| Chaos | Acts first, thinks never | Breaks everything | Joins fast for spectacle; least loyal |
| Optimist | Believes everyone is good | Easily manipulated | Open if they like founder; leaves if others harmed |
| Cynic | Assumes everything is doomed | Ruins happy moments | Mocks + counter-gossips; may infiltrate, turn sincere |
| Cryptic | Knows more than everyone | Never explains | Rarely joins openly; wildcard (advises, warns, knows things) |

Known dynamics: Planner vs Chaos · Optimist adopts Chaos · Cynic argues with Optimist · Cryptic randomly fixes problems · Everyone misunderstands Cryptic.
**Rule: tendencies only. No absolute responses.**

## Bubble Content Guidelines

- Voice: short, lowercase-casual, personality-flavored. "what's over here?" / "It was huge." / "Nothing today…" / "…Don't ring the fourth bell."
- Types: dialogue, reaction ("!", "ew", "♥"), gossip (references a real recent event), thought icon (intention).
- Duplicate suppression: same line/meaning ≤ once per several game days.
- Write ~10–15 lines per category per personality for MVP; expand from playtest gaps.
