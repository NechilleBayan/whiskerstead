DESIGN RULES FOR THIS BATCH (from files/01-cats.md + the design spec)
 - sleep_a/b: curled-up cozy ball, eyes closed. The two frames are a
   "boiling line" pair: SAME pose redrawn with a subtle breathing change,
   cycled slowly in-game.
 - collapsed: a hard design requirement says this must NEVER read as
   sleep. Splayed on its back/side, limbs out, eyes OPEN as small dazed
   dots, ears flat. Different silhouette from the sleep ball at 64 px.
 - Lying poses still anchor bottom-center: the body rests ON the same
   ground line where the standing cat's feet sit. No floating.
