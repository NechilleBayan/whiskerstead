 PROMPT 1 — tile_grass.png                                  canvas 512 x 512
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Seamlessly tileable terrain texture for a cozy hand-drawn doodle village
sim. Match the art style of the attached reference sprites — hand-drawn
doodle linework, flat slightly-desaturated fills, no gradients — but
this is BACKGROUND texture: it must stay far quieter than everything
drawn on top of it.

Subject: a soft meadow-green ground tile. Base is a flat fill of sage
green #c3d6b4, scattered with small hand-scribbled grass tufts (2–3
short curved pencil strokes each) in the slightly deeper green #b7cca7,
plus a few even subtler single-stroke flecks. Low contrast throughout —
the tufts should nearly disappear at a glance; charm shows only when you
look. No flowers, no stones, no dirt patches, no clover, no shadows, no
lighting variation.

Tiling requirements (critical): the image MUST tile seamlessly in all
directions — left edge continues the right edge exactly, top continues
bottom; no tuft may touch or cross any canvas edge unless it wraps
correctly to the opposite side; even, uniform tuft distribution with no
clusters, no bare "landmark" zones, and no brightness or hue gradient
across the tile (any of these becomes a visible repeating pattern when
tiled across the whole map).

Canvas 512 x 512 px, PNG, fully OPAQUE (this is the ground itself — no
transparency, full-bleed color to every edge). Flat colors only, no
texture noise, no photographic grain, no text, no watermark, no border.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
 PROMPT 2 — decal_path.png                                  canvas 256 x 256
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Terrain overlay decal for a cozy hand-drawn doodle village sim, matching
the attached reference sprites' hand-drawn doodle style — flat fills, no
gradients.

Subject: ONE straight segment of worn dirt path, drawn as a single
horizontal stroke across the middle of the canvas: dusty warm earth
brown #c2a97f with a few sparse darker speckles (#a8895e) suggesting
trodden ground. This one segment is the game's ENTIRE path system —
code chains it end to end and rotates it to turn corners — so both the
LEFT and RIGHT ends must be cleanly cut at the canvas edges with
matching height and shape (end-to-end copies must join without a bump),
while the long top and bottom edges are soft, irregular, wobbly
hand-drawn borders that fade slightly (light feathering) so the path
melts into grass when overlaid.

Path stroke ~70 px thick, centered vertically, running the full 256 px
width edge to edge. PNG RGBA: TRANSPARENT above and below the path,
opaque path body with softly feathered long edges only (the two cut
ends stay crisp and full-height). No grass drawn in this image, no
stones, no footprints, no text, no watermark, no shadow, no border.
